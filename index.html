About Us

We are a community of designers, thinkers, and advocates brought together by a shared love for the Passaic River ; as students of NJIT’s Master’s in Urban Design studio, we are not just studying cities, we are actively engaging with the landscapes that shape them. Our work extends beyond the classroom, driven by a commitment to understanding, reimagining, and caring for the environments we inhabit.

Our Goal

This exhibit is part of our ongoing exploration of the Passaic River—its history, its present challenges, and its future possibilities. Through design, research, and artistic expression, we aim to foster awareness and care for the river. We invite you to experience the river through a new lens, to reflect on the marks we leave behind, and to join us in shaping a more thoughtful relationship with the natural world.
